Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take?
The main program with mentoring can be completed over 5 weeks. There are 25 steps to progress through. On average: 2hrs scheduled mentoring call / 1.5hrs e-learning videos / 1.5hrs applying the tools per week.
How's it possible to save 35 years in retirement age?
35 years is based on the range we have seen so far from middle-aged program participants. We are seeing most participants improve their retirement trajectory by 15–20 years through better allocation, diversification, and leverage of untapped opportunities they didn't know existed.
Why create this program?
Jan discovered investing at 30 and achieved early retirement before 40 by applying innovation thinking to his financial strategy. After seeing how many smart professionals were stuck on autopilot with their finances, he built Plan B to share the frameworks that worked for him.
What's the approach of this program?
Plan B walks you through 25 critical steps that give you a helicopter view of investing. You'll assess your current trajectory, explore alternative asset classes, prototype new strategies, and build a personalized plan — all using design thinking methodology.
Why do good plans require desirability, viability and feasibility?
A robust financial plan must pass three tests: 1) Desirable — Do I actually want this life? 2) Viable — Is it realistically possible? 3) Feasible — Can I execute it with my resources and constraints? Most plans fail because they skip one of these dimensions.
What happens once I sign up?
You get immediate access to the LearnWorlds platform with all course materials. For cohort and concierge options, you'll receive a Zoom link for your mentoring sessions. You can start the self-paced content right away.
Do I need to invest any money during the program?
No. This is about investing into your education and building a plan. You won't be asked to put money into any investments during the program. The goal is to design your strategy first.
